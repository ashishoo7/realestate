Chintamanis- Where Luxury is 12ft High

At Chintamanis, we don’t just build spaces; we craft living environments that exceed expectations. Strategically positioned along the Dwarka Expressway, Chintamanis ensures effortless connectivity to Delhi and Gurgaon with direct entry and exit points.

12 feet Floor to Floor Height

Chintamanis epitomises luxury living within these refined condominiums. The exceptional feature of 12-foot ceilings shows unparalleled architectural magnificence which is the most unique and sought-after feature that Chintamanis offers. This effect of the 12 ft height is transformative. Not only does it give the condominiums a villa-like feel, but it also infuses them with a sense of majesty and tranquillity.

Lush Green Outdoors

Even though it is located in the heart of the NCR region, the outdoors gives the impression of a secluded green paradise. Chintamanis’ residences are possessed with the spirit of nature that ushers vitality, calmness and rejuvenation for both- the body and the soul. There are ample spaces for you to spend your early mornings and late evenings in the lap of nature.

Double Glazed Doors & Windows

Chintamanis residences feature double-glazed doors and windows that are a functionally eminent characteristic. The double-glazing provides superior insulation, regulating indoor temperatures and minimizing energy consumption. The muffling of sounds provided by the double-glazed windows adds to the exclusivity and provides silence and peace to the homeowners in their sanctuary.

Spacious Clubhouse

Chintamanis offers the residents an expansive clubhouse. This redefines the concept of community living here. The spaciousness of the clubhouse encourages gatherings, celebrations, and leisure pursuits. State-of-the-art amenities cater to diverse interests ensuring there is something for everyone. The clubhouse serves as the vibrant heart of the elite community while you live in the heart of the NCR region.

All-weather Indoor Swimming Pool

Chintamanis’ residences offer an All-Weather Indoor Swimming Pool. This is the epitome of luxury and convenience for all lovers of swimming and pool relaxation. The indoor feature ensures residents can enjoy the delights of swimming year-round, regardless of external conditions. Whether it’s a sunny day, rainy evening, or chilly winter morning- the climate-controlled environment provides a perfect spot for relaxation and fitness.

3X Car Parking Provision

At Chintamanis, we understand the importance of convenience, security and practical luxury. This is why we offer a generous 3x parking provision for all our residents. This thoughtful characteristic feature of Chintamanis ensures ample space for your vehicles- eliminating the hassle of searching for parking. Whether you’re a multi-car household or simply value the convenience of additional parking, we are dedicated to enhancing your lifestyle.
